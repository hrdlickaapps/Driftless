# Privacy Policy — Driftless

**Effective date:** March 14, 2026
**Developer:** Mike Hrdlicka

---

## The Short Version

Driftless does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There are no accounts, no ads, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

---

## What Data Driftless Uses

### Sleep Log
Driftless records basic listening session data on your device — the start time, duration, and sound you played. This data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and, if you have iCloud enabled, is synced privately across your own devices via Apple's CloudKit. **This data never leaves your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.**

### App Preferences
Driftless uses Apple's standard `UserDefaults` to remember your in-app settings (such as whether you've enabled sleep timer notifications). This data is stored locally on your device only.

### Notifications
If you enable sleep timer alerts, Driftless requests permission to send local notifications. No notification content is transmitted to any server.

### In-App Purchases
Premium features are purchased through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Driftless never sees or stores your payment information.

---

## What Driftless Does NOT Do

- Does not require an account or any personal information
- Does not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information
- Does not use analytics, crash reporting, or third-party tracking SDKs
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not sell or share data with third parties
- Does not access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera

---

## Children's Privacy

Driftless does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, it is suitable for all ages.
