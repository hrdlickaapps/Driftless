# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Driftless is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Driftless collects, uses, and protects your information.

**Summary: We do not collect personal data. Driftless is designed to be privacy-first.**

## 1. No Account Required

Driftless does not require you to create an account. You can use the app immediately without providing any personal information, email address, or login credentials.

## 2. What We Collect

### Sleep Log Data (CloudKit)
When you use the sleep timer or record sleep sessions, Driftless stores your sleep log data privately in Apple's CloudKit:

- Session start time and duration
- Which sound you listened to
- Whether the sleep timer ended naturally or was stopped manually

This data is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Stored in your iCloud container (private to your Apple ID)
- Never sent to our servers
- Deleted from iCloud only when you delete it from the app

### User Preferences (Local Storage)
Driftless stores your app preferences locally on your device using UserDefaults:

- Mixer slider positions
- Favorite soundscapes
- Notification preferences
- Timer settings

This data never leaves your device.

## 3. What We Don't Collect

Driftless does **not** collect:

- Your name, email address, or any personal identification
- Your location or geolocation data
- Your device advertising ID
- Your browsing history
- Your health data (beyond what you explicitly log in the app)
- Any data for analytics, tracking, or advertising purposes
- Crash logs or diagnostic data beyond what Apple's system frameworks provide
- Information about your contacts, photos, or other sensitive data

## 4. Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
Driftless uses Apple's services:

- **CloudKit** – Syncs your sleep log across your devices
- **StoreKit 2** – Processes in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
- **App Analytics** – Provides only aggregate, anonymized app performance data

Driftless does not send data to any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party trackers.

## 5. Required Permissions

Driftless requests the following permissions, all optional:

- **Notifications** – Only if you enable bedtime reminders or sleep timer alerts; used only for local, on-device notifications
- **iCloud/CloudKit** – Only to sync your sleep log (can be disabled in iOS Settings)
- **Audio** – Required to play sounds

You can disable any permission in iOS Settings without losing core functionality.

## 6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

- Apple's CloudKit encryption (AES-256)
- iOS's local data protection (when device is locked)
- No transmission to external servers
- No storage on our servers

## 7. Data Retention

- Sleep log data persists in iCloud until you delete it from the app or from iCloud Settings
- User preferences persist locally on your device until you delete the app
- Temporary data (active playback state) is never stored

You can delete all your data by:
- Deleting individual sleep sessions from the app
- Disabling CloudKit sync in iOS Settings
- Uninstalling the app

## 8. Children's Privacy

Driftless does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided information, we will take steps to delete such data immediately.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data (view it in the app or in iCloud Settings)
- Delete your data (from the app or iOS)
- Disable iCloud sync while continuing to use the app
- Disable any permission granted to Driftless

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact

If you have privacy concerns or questions about this policy, please contact us directly.

---

**Driftless respects your privacy.** We don't sell, share, or monetize your data. Your sleep log stays private, on your device and in your iCloud account.
